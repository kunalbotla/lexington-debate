Backers of Lexington Debate (BOLD) is a parent volunteer organization that supports the debate program at Lexington High School. Parents provide financial support through contributions and fundraising activities conducted by BOLD. Parents also support the team in hosting two large tournaments for competitors from other schools — one in November and one in January. Finally, parents can volunteer as judges or chaperones at tournaments that Lexington debaters attend at other schools.
